Hummus

Is a very popular Middle Eastern cuisine, usually eaten as a dip, with pita bread.

Ingredients:

  • Cooked chickpeas
  • Tahini 1/3 cup
  • Parsley
  • Lemon juice
  • Extra-virgin olive oil 2 tbs
  • Garlic
  • Salt, cumin powder

Recipe instructions:

  1. Peel the chickpeas after cooking them and then add them to the blender.
  2. Add all the ingredients to the blender and blend until it reaches a smooth consistency.
  3. Cover and refrigerate the hummus for an hour or so before serving.
  4. Transfer to a serving bowl and add a generous drizzle of good extra virgin olive oil. Add a few chickpeas to the middle, if you like. Sprinkle sumac on top.
